Spiral Chord

Spiral Chord of course is a Noise/Post-hardcore band hailing from Japan. The thing about these guys is that they are what you may call a dream team of musicians; Spiral Chord features some of the most recognizable names in the Japanese indie rock scene in it's lineup. You have Takebayashi Gendo from Cowpers as the vocalist/guitarist, Hera aka "junkhead herAx" from 200mph as the drummer, and finally Nakao Kentarou from Number Girl as the bassist. This makes for a interesting lineup that with out a doubt features some serious talent as well as chemistry.

Spiral Chord's sound is distinguished by Gendo's scratchy vocals, Nakao's pounding bass, and Hera's unrelenting cymbals and fast paced drumming topped off by the intentional underproduction that only enhances the garage punk feel. Spiral Chord songs are usually fast paced and high in energy with noisy guitars and atonial melodies.
